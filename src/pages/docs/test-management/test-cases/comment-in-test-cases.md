**Add comments to streamline communication and keep all test case discussions organized and accessible.**

---

When multiple team members contribute to the same test case, quick communication becomes essential. The commenting feature lets you discuss details, highlight issues, and exchange feedback directly in the test case. This article describes how to add comments and how to reply, react, or manage them. This article discusses adding comments in test cases. 

---

> ## **Prerequisites**
> 
> Before you begin, ensure that a test case exists or you know how to create one.

## **Add a Comment to a Test Case**

1. From the left navigation bar, go to **Test Cases**.

2. Open the test case where you want to add a comment.

3. In the **Activity** section, type your message in the comment field.

[[info | **NOTE**:]]
| You can tag a team member by typing **@** and selecting them from the list.

4. Click **Add Comment**.

5. Your comment appears in the **Activity** section.

---

## **Engage with Comments** 

1. Click the **Reply icon** to respond to an existing comment.

2. Click the **Emoji icon** to add a reaction.

3. Click the **Edit icon** to modify a comment, click **Update Comment**.
