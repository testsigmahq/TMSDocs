**Configure the Confluence integration in Test Management by Testsigma.**

---

Confluence is a team collaboration and knowledge management platform used to create, organize, and share documentation. The Confluence integration enables users to generate structured test cases directly from a Confluence page in Test Management by Testsigma.

---

> ## **Prerequisites**
> 
> Before you begin, ensure that you have an active Confluence account with the necessary permissions.

## **Integrate Confluence**

1. From the left navigation bar, go to **Settings > Integrations**.

2. Click **Setup Integration** next to the **Confluence** icon. This opens a new tab with the **Atlassian Login** page.

3. Log in using valid credentials.

4. On the **Requesting Access** page, click **Accept** to authorize the integration.
